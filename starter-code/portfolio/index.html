Hi, I'm Patrick

I'm a web developer based in Sydney Australia who finds joy in creating and discovering beautiful web designs.

I'm fascinated by innovative websites, product and user experiences.

My simple hope is to share my learnings and creativity with the world.

My journey

How did I get here?

"I completed my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering in 2011 and began a career in management consulting. However, I quickly fell in love with web technologies, user interfaces and human centered design principles. I realised I wanted to get involved directly in the creation of great user experiences on the web. This lead me to study UX Design in 2018 and to travel across the world in 2019 to Berlin to complete a course in Full Stack Web Development.

I'm now a proud Web Developer working in sunny Sydney Australia."
