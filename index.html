My journey

How did I get here?

I completed my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering in 2016 and immediately began a career in management consulting. I worked on large business transformation projects but always found myself drawn towards the digital space. I quickly realised I was hungry to use my creative skills in my career and fell in love with software programming.

I wanted to get involved directly in the creation of great user experiences with software. This lead me to study UX Design in 2018 and to travel across the world in 2019 to Berlin to complete a course in Full Stack Web Development.

I'm now a proud Web Developer working in sunny Sydney Australia.
